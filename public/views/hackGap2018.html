Hack the Gap 2018 marked the first hackathon in which I've ever participated.  I didn't really know what to expect, and was just a little terrified going into it as a novice programmer.  Once I got there, I realized it was going to be a great weekend.

Our group built an Game Time, which is meant to help schedule and organize a large group for a day of board gaming.  Want to see how it works?  Follow the Game Time link.  All of the demos from that weekend are also available here.

Would I do it again?  In a heartbeat.
